VulcanAMI is a project that explores governed training for language models.
It integrates a consensus engine to review and approve weight updates.
The training loop includes safety checks, rollback, and checkpointing.
Graphiix IR provides a foundation for model representation and execution.
Retrieval augmented generation can add documents at question time without retraining.
Small datasets are useful for quick experiments and debugging the training pipeline.
Cosine learning rate scheduling with warmup is common for transformer models.
Evaluation on a validation split helps detect overfitting and plateaus early.
Curriculum strategies can start easier and gradually increase difficulty.
This tiny corpus is only for demonstration and wiring tests, not for real accuracy.
