# 🗺️ Unflattenable Roadmap – Preserving Hierarchy & Analog Semantics

**Version:** 2.2.0

---

## 1. Concept

“Unflattenable” design resists collapsing multi-level, heterogeneous computation into a purely uniform digital abstraction. It preserves:

- Hierarchical graph layering
- Distributed execution semantics
- Analog / photonic variability
- Semantic traceability & provenance

Objective: Minimize information loss and optimize sustainability & cognitive fidelity.

Hardening, sustainability metrics, optimization leveraging learned subgraphs.

## 5. Design Principles

| Principle | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------|
| Hierarchical Preservation | Avoid oversimplification of multi-stage reasoning |
| Analog Fidelity | Capture non-digital performance variance |
| Semantic Layering | Track conceptual transformations, not just raw ops |
| Deterministic Auditing | Immutable execution & proposal lineage |
| Energy Awareness | Sustainable scaling under constrained resources |
