## What is VulcanAMI_LLM?

Graphix Vulcan is an **AI-native graph execution and governance platform** that enables enterprises to:
- Build and execute complex AI workflows as verifiable, typed graphs
- Govern AI system changes through trust-weighted consensus
- Ensure safety and compliance with built-in alignment checks
- Monitor and audit all AI operations with cryptographic provenance

**Think:** "Kubernetes for AI Governance" or "Airflow meets AI Safety"
